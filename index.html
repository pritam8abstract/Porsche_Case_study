The Strategic Advantages

  • Economies of Scope: Shared R&D infrastructure across all projects created knowledge accumulation impossible for single-focus competitors
  • Revenue Diversification: When luxury car sales fell during recessions, engineering contracts (counter-cyclically) increased as companies outsourced to cut costs
  • Innovation Pipeline: Hybrid tech from client projects ‚Üí Porsche hybrids; material science from industrial apps ‚Üí better vehicles
  • Talent Magnet: Engineers wanted to work where they touched elite sports cars AND diverse industrial challenges

McKinsey & Company. (2007). Diversification strategies in automotive manufacturing. Quarterly Insights Report.

But here's the paradox: PEG's largest client was Volkswagen‚Äîproviding ~40% of capacity. This symbiotic relationship was about to become... complicated.
