The Ideological Crossroads

Is AI the "Ultimate Capitalism" or a trigger for forced structural change?

Ultimate Capitalism

Capitalism seeks to lower the cost of production. AI offers Zero Marginal Cost labor.

If corporations can generate value without paying wages, profit is maximized, but who buys the products? This is the paradox of "Hyper-Efficiency."

The "Invading" Ideology

Do we need "invading left ideologies" to forcefully stop technology?

Calls for Universal Basic Income (UBI), Robot Taxes, and strict regulation are rising. Without these "socialist" safety nets, the technological displacement might lead to societal collapse.
